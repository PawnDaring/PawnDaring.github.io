About Me

Welcome to JCR Studios, where creativity meets code! I'm a self-taught coder with three years of experience dabbling in JavaScript, Python, HTML, and CSS. But that's just the tip of the iceberg. I'm also a creative problem solver, a marketing mad scientist, and a deep thinker. I love building tools that help others—though honestly, I do it just as much because it’s fun.

This site is my experimental playground, a mix of passion projects, quirky ideas, and attempts to make something uniquely mine. Let's explore and have some fun!
